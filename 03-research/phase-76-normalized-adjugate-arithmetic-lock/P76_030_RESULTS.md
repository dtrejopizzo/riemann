# P76.030 - Bilateral characteristic results

At fixed `lambda=6`, `N=6,...,12`, comparison with normalized `Xi^2` gives:

```text
real relative L2:   0.3617 -> 0.2092
real relative sup:  0.3595 -> 0.1993
Euler-safe max rel: 0.1980 -> 0.0792
```

All three errors decrease monotonically.  The bilateral construction is
exactly even and preserves finite real-rootedness; unlike taking the even
part of a unilateral characteristic, it does not alter the divisor by
addition.

These results support the object but do not prove the cofinal Euler-safe
limit.  P76.031 shows that this one limit is now sufficient for Omega7.
