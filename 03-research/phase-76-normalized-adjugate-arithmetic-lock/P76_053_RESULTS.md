# P76.053 - Directional weight audit

At `z_0=i,z=2i`, the numerator `delta r dot w` has cancellation factors
between `8.2` and `9.8`.  Its absolute mass is concentrated in low modes:
for `N=8`,

```text
total absolute mass: 2.73e-2
low-mode mass:       2.70e-2
high-mode mass:      2.79e-4
new-boundary mass:   7.07e-9.
```

Therefore `SM-SHELL` cannot be proved by claiming that the selected inverse
weight localizes at the shell.  Its smallness is a signed low-mode Cauchy
cancellation.
