# P76.052 - Sherman--Morrison scalar certification

The identity `(SM-7)` closes with relative errors `1e-90--1e-81`.
For `sigma=0.6,1.5,2` and `N=5,...,8`,

```text
N^2 |theta_N|/L^2 <=0.074.
```

The scalar itself decreases from at most `0.0377` at `N=5` to `0.0148` at
`N=8`.  This independently reproduces the stable shell cross-ratio law and
isolates the exact directional quantity that must be bounded.
