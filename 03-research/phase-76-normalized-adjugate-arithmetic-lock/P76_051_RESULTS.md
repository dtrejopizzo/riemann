# P76.051 - Jacobi complementary-minor certification

The identity

```text
[T_{N+1}(z)/T_N(z)]/[T_{N+1}(z_0)/T_N(z_0)]
=det R_N(z_0)/det R_N(z)
```

was verified for `N=5,...,8` and `sigma=0.6,1.5,2`.  Relative errors range
from `7.6e-89` to `1.5e-81`.

This replaces the cancellation of ambient terms as large as `1e7` by an
exact normalized determinant of size two.  No eigenvalue, inverse-gap norm
or fitted amplitude enters the identity.
