
Therefore the theorem is not a cell-functional identity on `ker Q`.  It is
specific to the pole-even eigenline.  The proof must use `(H_L-mu_LI)xi_L=0`
in an essential way, but without using `Qxi_L` as small input.


of `Y` is forbidden; `Y` must come from the finite cell/Hilbert-harmonic
algebra.
