## 2. The honest pattern, and what it means

Across phase 6 (and consistent with P8 Theorem C, M1, and all of route B): **every attractive reduction that
seemed to give leverage on the *sign* collapsed, on careful computation, back to the cross-place conspiracy
(archimedean vs. the indefinite primes) — i.e. to the known wall.** The OS recasting is correct and pretty;
its mechanism is refuted. The incoherence/large-values picture is the *right frontier* but is **not a new
sound reduction** — it is the standard $S(T)$ / large-values story, itself open and RH-tied, and our attempt
to make it a pointwise reduction was wrong.

**This is the discipline working, not failing.** Catching two flawed reductions in one session is the system
doing its job. But it also forces an honest conclusion the program must state plainly:

> **We do not currently have a sound new path across the wall.** The directions our work opens (Connes' KMS
> per-place positivity; the large-values/$S(T)$ frontier) are precisely the field's known open cores. Our
> genuine assets (the $\omega$-class interference machinery, the validated detector) are pointed at the right
> place (large values) but do not, as far as this session's rigorous work shows, yield a crossing — and
> manufacturing further plausible-looking framings would be motivated reasoning, not mathematics.

## 4. The only honest options from here

1. **Stop generating framings; consolidate.** The harvest (P9 + detector + the no-go map) is the real output;
   package it for the record. *(Recommended if the goal is durable, defensible results.)*
2. **Engage the actual open core (low odds, eyes fully open):** Connes' KMS/Bost–Connes per-place positivity,
   or the large-values frontier with the $\omega$-class machinery — knowing these are the field's hard,
   open problems and that we have no special key, only a good vantage. *(Choose only with realistic
   expectations: months of work, $<1\%$ of RH, but possibly a real partial result on large values or B2.)*
3. **Accept the honest verdict:** the sign requires an idea neither we nor the field has in 2026; the
   computational certainty of RH does not translate, with current mathematics, into a pure-math proof, and our
   program has mapped *why* with unusual precision.

**The faithful-log entry is this:** *We tried to cross the wall with a genuinely new synthesis (OS reflection
positivity via the Euler-product lattice). The recasting is correct; the mechanism is refuted by direct
computation; a follow-up reduction was flawed and retracted. We have no crossing. The durable outputs are
P9, the validated detector, and a precise, audited map of exactly why the sign resists — which is, honestly,
where the value of this program lives.*
