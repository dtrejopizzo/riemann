## Finite multiplicative group algebra

Let

```text
Gamma_x = < n : n=p^m<=x >
```

be the finite-rank free abelian multiplicative group generated by the primes up to `x`.

For each block `j=0,1`, define the operator-valued group-algebra element

```text

sum_{n in S_j(x)}
```

```text

A_n^- = - beta_n C_x^(-1/2)B_x^*U_{log n}^*B_xC_x^(-1/2).
```

The augmentation map is

```text

```

Then

```text

```

## Coefficient identity

For every `r>=1`,

```text
mathcal A_j^r = sum_{R in Gamma_x} C_{j,r}(R) delta_R,
```

where the operator coefficient is

```text
C_{j,r}(R)
 =
sum_{prod n_i^eps_i=R}
A_{n_1}^{eps_1}...A_{n_r}^{eps_r}.
```

Taking trace and augmentation gives the exact moment identity

```text
Tr(K_j^r)
 =
sum_{R in Gamma_x} Tr C_{j,r}(R).                 (GA-MOM)
```

This is the exact version of the displacement decomposition.
