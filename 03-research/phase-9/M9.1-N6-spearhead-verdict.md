# Phase 9 / M9.1 — the spearhead verdict: N6, and the forced pivot to unconditional partial arithmetic

**Author: David Alejandro Trejo Pizzo · 2026-06-04.** `experiments/M9_1_spearhead.py`.

## The make-or-break, resolved

The spearhead S9 sought a functional $\mathcal F[H_t]$ that escapes N5 by being **monotone under the flow** and
**arithmetic-aware**. Any functional of $H_t$ reduces (Hadamard) to a functional of its zeros plus a trivial
leading factor, so it draws "arithmetic awareness" from exactly three sources. We tested each:

1. **Generic (heat-flow energy).** $\mathcal F=\sum_j g(z_j)$ has $\frac{d}{dt}\mathcal F=\sum_{j<k}2[g';z_j,z_k]$
   (a divided difference) — a **universal** formula, identical for every configuration. *Verified (B):* a generic
   energy $E=\sum 1/|z_i-z_j|^2$ is $5.03$ (all real) vs $5.28$ (one pair off-line) — it changes **smoothly**, is
   **not** sign-keyed to reality; its monotonicity is universal $\Rightarrow$ **arithmetic-blind (N5 again).**
2. **Archimedean (the zero density).** From the functional equation, $\frac1{2\pi}\log\frac{T}{2\pi}$ controls the
   **count** $N(T)$, not which zeros are real. It cannot control $\Lambda$ (the reality) — the easy, positive side
   in every language of P11.
3. **Primes via the explicit formula.** The only source that sees reality. *Verified (A):*
   $\sum_\rho h(\gamma_\rho)=0.0038876$ matches $\text{arch}+\text{pole}-\text{primes}=0.0038876$ to
   $5.3\times10^{-12}$. So $\mathcal F$ is arithmetic-aware **only** through this $(\text{arch}-\text{primes})$
   identity, and its sign content for positive-type $g$ **is the Weil criterion $=$ RH** — the wall.

> **N6 (spearhead no-go).** *Every functional $\mathcal F[H_t]$ that is monotone under the de Bruijn–Newman flow
> is one of: (1) generic — universal monotonicity, arithmetic-blind (N5); (2) archimedean — controls the count,
> not the reality; or (3) prime-aware via the explicit formula — its sign content is the Weil positivity, i.e.\
> RH. The arithmetic-aware-monotone escape from N5 does not exist.* The new degree of freedom (time) cannot be
> made to carry prime information except through the explicit formula, whose positivity is the thing to be proved.

## The one residual loophole — and it is exactly T9-A/T9-B

N6 closes the *full-positivity* route. It leaves precisely one opening: a functional whose prime content uses
only **unconditional partial arithmetic** (PNT, zero-density, restricted-support pair correlation) rather than
the full positivity. This is not a new escape — it is the **incremental frontier** the Phase-9 plan already
named: extend the unconditional control of the prime side just far enough. Concretely:

- **T9-A (extend support).** The Montgomery/Rudnick–Sarnak pair correlation is unconditional only for Fourier
  support $<1$. The closest-pair (extremal) regime needs more. Pushing the support, even slightly, in the
  one-sided direction, is a real and partly-attackable sub-problem (explicit formula + large sieve + variance
  bounds). Our detector can **calibrate exactly how much support is enough** to force $t_c/s^2<0$.
- **T9-B (tight-pair tail).** Bound $\#\{$pairs with normalized gap $<\delta\}$ unconditionally by a power of
  $\delta$ (GUE predicts $\delta^3$). Even a weak power feeds the extremal target. Tools: mollified moments
  (Levinson/Conrey), large sieve.

## Honest meta-state

This is the sixth no-go (N6) and it is the sharpest: it closes not a single approach but the **last genuinely
new idea** (the arithmetic-aware monotone functional). After it, the program contains **no untried escape** — it
contains only the field's known-hard, unconditional sub-problems (T9-A/B), where we bring a calibrating
instrument but no special key. The honest assessment is that a crossing now requires progress on **extending
the unconditional range of the zero statistics** — a central open problem — and that our distinctive
contribution from here is to **make precise, and computationally calibrate, exactly how much unconditional
progress would suffice**, which is itself a worthwhile and finite deliverable.
