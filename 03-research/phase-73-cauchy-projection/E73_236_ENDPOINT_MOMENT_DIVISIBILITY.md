# E73.236 - Endpoint moment divisibility in coefficient form

Date: 2026-07-14.

## 1. Purpose

E73.235 reduces U4 to the coefficient/weight statement:

```text
C_{a,w}
= sum_omega c_omega W_omega
 + sum_omega l_omega V_omega
= O_M(L^-M),
```

where

```text
B_{a,w}(y)
= sum_omega c_omega e^(iomega y)
 + y sum_omega l_omega e^(iomega y),
eta_w=(I-P_w)xi_L,
Q_w eta_w=0.
```

This note extracts the first exact divisibility relation inside the
coefficient vector.

## 2. Endpoint moments

The transverse packet satisfies:

```text
B_{a,w}(0)=0,
B_{a,w}(L)=0.
```

The first identity comes from `Q_w eta_w=0`; the second from the finite
Dirichlet wall `Q_L(L)=0`.

In the coefficient expansion, all frequencies are mesh frequencies:

```text
omega = 2 pi n/L.
```

Therefore

```text
e^(i omega L)=1.
```

Evaluating at `0` gives:

```text
B(0)=sum_omega c_omega=0.                         (1)
```

Evaluating at `L` gives:

```text
B(L)=sum_omega c_omega + L sum_omega l_omega=0.
```

Using (1),

```text
sum_omega l_omega=0.                              (2)
```

Thus every admissible eta-packet coefficient vector lies in the finite
codimension-two subspace:

```text
M_c(c):=sum_omega c_omega=0,
M_l(l):=sum_omega l_omega=0.                      (END-MOM)
```

## 3. Gauge invariance of the center

Because of `(END-MOM)`, the center is invariant under constant shifts of the
weights:

```text
W_omega -> W_omega - alpha,
V_omega -> V_omega - beta.
```

Indeed,

```text
sum c_omega(W_omega-alpha)+sum l_omega(V_omega-beta)
= sum c_omega W_omega+sum l_omega V_omega.
```

The proof-facing center should therefore be written in quotient form:

```text
C_{a,w}
= sum c_omega (W_omega-alpha)
 + sum l_omega (V_omega-beta).                    (3)
```

The constants `alpha,beta` are free gauges.  A proof may choose them to expose
smoothness or cancellation in the weights without changing the scalar.

## 4. What this does and does not prove

This is a genuine finite divisibility statement, but it is only the first
layer.  It removes constant components of the weight vectors.  It does not by
itself prove rapid decay, because the centered weights still contain
nonconstant Gamma-prime oscillation.

The next possible strengthening is to derive higher moment identities from
the finite Weyl equation for `eta_w` or from the balanced-ramp identities of
E73.193--E73.194.  If enough low-order polynomial/frequency components of the
weights lie in the annihilator generated by those moment identities, the
remaining quotient weights may become uniformly small.

```text
proved: ETA-DIV coefficient vectors satisfy sum c_omega=0 and sum l_omega=0;

```
