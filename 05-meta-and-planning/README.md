# Meta and Planning

Working documents that sit *around* the research, not inside it: planning notes, strategy
memos, cross-cutting audits, and the discriminator machinery used to triage candidate
approaches. **These are not papers and not phase results** — they are the scaffolding that
guided the program. They are kept for traceability (so a reader can reconstruct *why* a
direction was chosen or dropped) but are not part of the publishable corpus.

For the polished outputs, see [`../04-papers/`](../04-papers/); for the phase-by-phase
research record, see [`../03-research/`](../03-research/).
